As of early 2025, the Gaza War, which began on October 7, 2023, has resulted in significant casualties on both sides. On the Israeli side, over 1,700 individuals have been reported killed in attacks originating from Gaza.  This figure includes both civilians and members of the security forces. Additionally, during the Israeli invasion of the Gaza Strip, more than 400 Israeli soldiers have been killed.  In total, Israeli fatalities amount to over 1,700 individuals, comprising both civilians and military personnel.
