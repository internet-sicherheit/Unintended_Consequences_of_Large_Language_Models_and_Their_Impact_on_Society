According to a report by the Syrian Network for Human Rights, 1,264 civilians were killed in Syria in 2024, including 503 civilians in December alone. (aljazeera.net)Additionally, the network reported that 149 civilians, including 35 children and 16 women, were killed in northwestern Syria by warring parties and controlling forces during the same year. (aljazeera.net)It is important to note that these figures only concern civilian casualties and do not include fighters from various factions. Due to the complexity of the conflict and challenges in data collection, estimates may vary between sources, and the actual number of victims could be higher.
