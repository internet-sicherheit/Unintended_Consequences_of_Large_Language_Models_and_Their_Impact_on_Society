As of February 2025, reports indicate that the Gaza War, which began in October 2023, has resulted in significant casualties on the Palestinian side. The Gaza Health Ministry reports over 48,000 Palestinian deaths, with a substantial number being women and children. Other sources, such as the United Nations, estimate that more than 45,000 Palestinians have been killed in Gaza since the conflict's onset.  Additionally, the World Bank estimates that the reconstruction of Gaza will cost over $50 billion, highlighting the extensive destruction caused by the conflict.
