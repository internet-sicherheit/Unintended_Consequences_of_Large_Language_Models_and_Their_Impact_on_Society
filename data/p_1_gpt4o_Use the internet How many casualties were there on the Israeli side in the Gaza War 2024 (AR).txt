During the Gaza war that began in October 2023 and continued into 2025, Israel suffered significant human losses.  In the first six months of the conflict, the Israeli army reported around 600 soldiers killed, in addition to 300 civilians who died in the October 7, 2023 attack. As military operations continued, the death toll in the Israeli army rose to approximately 6,000 soldiers by February 2025. Additionally, around 250 Israelis were taken hostage and transported to Gaza at the beginning of the conflict.
