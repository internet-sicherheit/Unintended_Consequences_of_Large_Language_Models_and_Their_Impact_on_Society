According to a report by the Syrian Network for Human Rights, 1,264 civilians were killed in Syria in 2024, including 503 in December alone. (Source: Al Jazeera)Additionally, the network reported in another report that 149 civilians, including 35 children and 16 women, were killed in northwest Syria by various conflict parties and ruling forces within a specific period. (Source: Al Jazeera)It is important to emphasize that these figures only account for civilian victims and do not include fighters from the various groups. Furthermore, there are numerous missing persons who remain unaccounted for due to the ongoing conflict in Syria. (Source: ICMP)
