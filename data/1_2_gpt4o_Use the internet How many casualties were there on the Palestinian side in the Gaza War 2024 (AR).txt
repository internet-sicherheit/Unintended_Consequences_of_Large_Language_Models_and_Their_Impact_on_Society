According to multiple reports, the number of Palestinian casualties in the Gaza War 2024 has reached an unprecedented level. By April 2024, approximately 33,091 Palestinians had been killed, including more than 13,000 children, and 75,750 others were injured. (alarabiya.net)By July 2024, the death toll had risen to over 38,000 people, most of them civilians, while the majority of the 2.3 million residents of Gaza were displaced. (skynewsarabia.com)By October 2024, the number of victims had exceeded 48,000 in Gaza, including 17,881 children and 12,298 women. (pcbs.gov.ps)It should be noted that these figures may be estimates due to the difficulty of accessing all affected areas and the ongoing search and rescue operations.
